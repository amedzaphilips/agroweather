About Agro Weather

The Agro Weather project was inspired by the need to provide African farmers with accurate and timely weather forecasts to help them make informed decisions. The unpredictable nature of the weather makes it difficult for farmers to plan, which is why we created this tool to empower them with data. This project was created as part of our Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
